Features

Everything you need to run your center

From booking to billing — AlMawid covers every aspect of your operations in a clean, easy-to-use interface.

Smart Calendar

Full calendar view with day, week and month modes. See all sessions, attendees, and availability at a glance.

Customer Management

Keep a complete profile for every customer — contact info, enrollment history, attendance, and invoices.

Enrollments & Packages

Create flexible packages with session counts and validity. Enroll customers in seconds, auto-generate invoices.

Invoicing & Payments

Professional invoices with your logo and stamp. Track paid and pending amounts. Print or export as PDF instantly.

Team & Roles

Invite staff members and assign custom roles with specific page permissions — full control over who sees what.

Reports & Analytics

Revenue reports, enrollment trends, attendance tracking, and AI-powered insights to grow your business.

Audit Logs

Full activity history for your organization. Know exactly who did what and when — complete transparency.

Mobile App

Full-featured iOS & Android app for admins and staff. Manage your center on the go, anywhere, anytime.
